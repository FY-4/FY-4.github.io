Question && Answer

What needs to be done before using the equipment for the first time? +
Before first use, please:
1. Read the instruction manual
2. Check all fasteners
3. Fully charge the battery
4. Test the seeding and fertilizing mechanisms.。
How do you clean and maintain the equipment? +
Clean the seeder and fertilizer applicator after each use, regularly check the belt tension, and clean the water tank and pipes monthly.
How should the planting spacing be adjusted? +
Planting spacing is fixed at 15cm. If adjustment is needed, replace gears with different tooth counts. Refer to the technical manual for specific methods.
How long is the battery life of the device? +
Under standard working conditions, the 5300mAh battery can support continuous operation for 8-10 hours. Charging takes approximately 4-5 hours.
